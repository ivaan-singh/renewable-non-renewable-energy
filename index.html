  1. Definitions

  • Renewable Energy - energy from a source that is not depleted when used.
  • Non-Renewable Energy - energy from a source that is depleted when used.

  1. Renewable Energy

  • Solar Energy
  • Hydro Energy
  • Ocean Energy
  • Biomass Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Geothermal Energy

  1. Non-Renewable Energy

  • Coal
  • Gas
  • Oil
  • CNG / LPG
  • Uranium
